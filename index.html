Critical Role

Critical Role is a web series produced by Geek & Sundry which is broadcast live on Twitch.tv on Thursday nights at 8pm (Mountain Time). The show features a group of professional voice actors and follows them as they play through a Dungeons & Dragons campaign. Each episode is available through Geek & Sundry's Critical Role page and Youtube channel at a later date (they typically upload on monday afternoon).


Dungeons & Dragons?

If you don't know anything about Dungeons & Dragons it is a fantasy based table-top RPG (role-playing-game) which maintains a basic structure of a leader known as the Dungeon Master (DM) who guides and narrates and referees the rest of the players through a fantasy adventure. Various polyhedral dice are used to generate the outcomes of actions taken by players as well as actions taken by the DM. Learn more about D&D and check out the official site.

You don't have to know anything about D&D to enjoy Critical Role, in fact, I barely knew anything about it before I got hooked on this show. The storytelling, improvisation and genuine fun are the biggest factors for why I love Critical Role. Check it out for yourself!!

Extra Nerdy Extras

There is a massive community of fans of Critical Role! Followers have adopted the name "critters". There are many fans who are extremely talented artists and creators who generate loads of fan art every week. Before every episode is broadcast a stream of the previous weeks' fan art submitions is aired. Following the hashtag #criticalrolefanart on Twitter and visiting the Fan Art page on Geek & Sundry are great places to see some beautiful examples. There is also a spin off show called Talks Machina, which is hosted by Brian Wayne Foster, also produced and accessable at Geek & Sundry. Talks Machina streams live on Tuesday nights on Twitch.tv at 8pm MT.
